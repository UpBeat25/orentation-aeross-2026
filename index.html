About

About

CelesteCon 2026 is AEROSS's flagship national inter-school aerospace competition — a convergence of science, innovation, and imagination hosted at DPS RK Puram, New Delhi.

Now in its - edition, CelesteCon brings together the brightest student minds from across India to compete, collaborate, and push the boundaries of space science, rocketry, and engineering. Nine meticulously designed events challenge participants across technical, creative, and analytical dimensions.

Whether you're a seasoned aerospace enthusiast or a curious first-timer, CelesteCon is your launchpad.
